Discover Hokkaido Powder Belt

The Hokkaido Powder Belt is a premier destination for skiing enthusiasts, renowned for its exceptional powder snow, diverse ski resorts, and easy accessibility. Located in the central region of Hokkaido, this area stretches approximately 200 kilometers from north to south, offering a variety of skiing experiences across several prominent ski resorts.

Exceptional Snow Quality

Thanks to Hokkaido's unique climate, the area boasts some of the world's driest and lightest powder snow. Low humidity and temperatures often below -20°C ensure perfect skiing conditions throughout the season.

Convenient Access

The region is easily accessible from New Chitose Airport, which serves as a gateway to Hokkaido, offering an array of shopping, dining options, and direct international flights.

Variety of Activities

Apart from skiing, the Hokkaido Powder Belt offers a rich après-ski scene with local dining, Ice Village, and luxurious accommodations. Resorts like Hoshino Resorts TOMAMU provide unique attractions such such as Mina-Mina Beach(Largest indoor pool in Japan) and Ice Bar in the Ice Village, ensuring an exciting stay.

Experience unparalleled snow quality, diverse ski options, and vibrant après-ski activities in the stunning Hokkaido Powder Belt.
